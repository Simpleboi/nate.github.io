About Me

Highly focused and ambitious, I bring a rich background of intermediate software engineering, encompassing over two years of hands-on experience in Python and C++, soon to include a web development skill set with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. If I'm not programming applications, I'm programming music. Every week, I transform my musical sound into something I've never done before. I'm on a dual journey to obtain loyal fans and craft a community-adored game engine. Join me as I weave programming and music into my evolving professional identity.

Programming

After several tech demos that extend my graphics programming knowledge in both the Python and C++ languages, I initiated the development of my own game engine. It is a library that extends and wraps SDL2 with integral features such as bitwise collision detection, dummy physics, pathfinding, and texture cache management. I call it Kraken Engine.

Game Dev

I am the developer behind local hit games such as Mage and Muse and Night Terror, both created in under a week.

Music

My music as of now is heavily influenced by big artists such as The Weeknd, Tory Lanez, NF, Bruno Mars, d4vd, and Q. The genre I'm currenly working on is electronic music with an 80s-90s structure where I make use of my musical and technical skills to engineer synthesizers to make unique sounds.
